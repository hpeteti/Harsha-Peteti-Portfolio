Description

  • Analyzed sales data for Atliq Hardware, addressing a significant decline in sales across regions, focusing on key metrics such as revenue breakdown by city, product profitability, and customer trends.
  • Created two comprehensive dashboards in Tableau to track revenue, profit margins, and sales patterns, providing actionable insights for Bhavin Patel, the Sales Director.
  • Identified Delhi NCR as the top revenue generator, while Surat emerged as the most profitable market with a 4.9% profit margin.
  • Uncovered that 75.6% of sales came from Brick & Mortar stores, with E-Commerce accounting for the remaining 24.4%, offering insights into sales channels.
  • Highlighted a sharp sales decline starting in Q2 of 2019, where revenue dropped from 86.9 million to 63.2 million by Q2 2020, with profit margins plummeting to 0.9%.
  • Gained proficiency in data manipulation, visualization, and SQL through this project, presenting key business metrics using advanced Tableau techniques.
