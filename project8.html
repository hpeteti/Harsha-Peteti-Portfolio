Description

  • Conducted an in-depth sales analysis for Adidas using Tableau, exploring various retail KPIs and sales performance indicators to uncover critical insights into trends, regional performance, and product profitability.
  • Analyzed a dataset comprising retailer information, sales details, product attributes, and financial metrics such as operating profit and margin, enabling a comprehensive overview of sales dynamics.
  • Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) using histograms, scatter plots, box plots, and heatmaps to reveal patterns and correlations between sales performance and market demographics.
  • Leveraged geospatial analysis to visualize the geographic distribution of Adidas sales, identifying high-performing regions and gaining actionable insights on state-level sales trends.
  • Developed interactive Tableau dashboards to showcase sales trends over time, price range analysis, and retailer performance, providing stakeholders with valuable decision-making tools.
  • Discovered that New York leads in sales, while products priced at $55 generated the highest revenue across all categories.
  • Identified Men’s Street Footwear as the most profitable product in the Northeast region, enhancing strategic planning for regional marketing and sales initiatives.
  • Highlighted North Carolina’s top-selling products, revealing Women’s Apparel and Men’s Street Footwear as key drivers of profitability, with July being the month with the highest sales.
